Hogwarts School!

Welcome to Hogwarts school of Witchcraft and Wizardy! Hogwarts was founded by two wizard and two witches: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, or Slytherin. The houses are named after them. They each represented an aspect of personality that they wanted to bring out in new students. Each student will be diveded into four different houses depending on their personality.In Hogwarts, you can learn to do all kind of magics and tricks. You will gain the knowledge to use your power to do magics. you will be able to utilize that knowledge and do real magic. So Be Prepared and explore while you learn!

As you know, with the new technology you will be assigned to your house by just entering your name and It will display your name with the house.
